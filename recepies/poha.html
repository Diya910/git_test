Poha Recepie

Poha is a popular Indian dish made from flattened rice, often flavored with mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, and other spices. It is a quick and nutritious breakfast or snack, commonly enjoyed for its light and fluffy texture.

INGREDIENTS:

  • flatenned rice
  • peanuts
  • water
  • spices
  • oil
    •

STEPS

  1. Rinse Poha: Rinse 1 cup of Poha (flattened rice) under running water. Gently wash and soak it for about 10 minutes. Drain excess water.
  2. Prepare Seasoning: In a pan, heat oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions are golden brown.
  3. Add Vegetables and Poha: Add diced potatoes (optional) and peas to the pan. Cook until they are tender. Then, add the soaked Poha to the pan.
  4. Season with Spices: Sprinkle turmeric powder, salt, and sugar over the Poha. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes until the Poha is heated through.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Garnish the Poha with chopped coriander leaves and a squeeze of lime juice. Serve it hot, optionally with sev (crispy chickpea noodles) on top.
